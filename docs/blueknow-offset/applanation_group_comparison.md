# Concentric vs Offset 0.5 mm Applanation Comparison

The offset case is 16a, confirmed from Body4 center X=0.5 mm.
The concentric case is 07_concentric_contact_all_rerun, confirmed from Body4 center X=0.0 mm.

Offset 0.5 mm measured area ratio in the 0.20-0.32 mm window: 1.436 +/- 0.007.
Concentric 0.0 mm rerun stores contact output, but the Body2-Body3 inner interface CONT,PRES field is zero in all five frames.
Therefore a pressure-equivalent inner area coefficient is not reported for this older concentric MVP model.
